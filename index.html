--- title: Home heading: Welcome to the Rocky Mountain Mutual Aid Network (RMMAN)! ---

RMMAN is a network of Coloradans providing direct aid and assistance to those who need it most during these challenging times. Our platform makes it easy and rewarding to directly help those in need and to receive aid and support yourself!

What is ‘mutual aid’? How is it different from charity or government assistance?

Please watch this 3-minute video for a quick overview. The key is that mutual aid is based on the fact that we are all interdependent on one another in a society. Therefore we should seek to provide aid to those who need it when we have the means and, in turn, to seek aid from those in our community when we need a little help ourselves. We can do this ourselves! There doesn’t need to be a government, bureaucracy or tax-exempt non-profit organizing this work. We can build organizations to do this work and to empower our communities to help one another! RMMAN strives to be an example of this.
