Start a Pod

We want you to start a pod! A pod is any small group of people who meet regularly for a purpose. That purpose could be anything. Be it mutual aid, education, activism, emotional support, deep community building, learning something, sustainability, environmental repair, or any other purpose we want to help you get started.
